About VedaRoots

VedaRoots is India's most comprehensive digital encyclopedia of Ayurvedic and medicinal plants. We bridge the gap between ancient healing wisdom and modern technology, making the knowledge of 5,000 years of Ayurveda accessible to everyone.

Our platform combines detailed botanical profiles, interactive tools, regional plant mapping, and user-friendly features to help students, practitioners, and curious learners explore the incredible world of herbal medicine.
